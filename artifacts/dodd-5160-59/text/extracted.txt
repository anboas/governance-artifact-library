                                                                                 NUMBER 5160.59
                                                                                      April 14, 2004
                                                            Certified Current as of October 30, 2006

                                                                                            GC, DOD

SUBJECT: Delegation of Authority Under Executive Order 11390

References: (a) DoD Directive 5160.59, "Delegation of Authority Under Executive Order
                11390, dated 22 January 1968," January 22, 1968 (hereby canceled)
            (b) Executive Order 11390, "Delegation of Certain Functions of the President to the
                Secretary of Defense," January 22, 1968

1. REISSUANCE AND PURPOSE

   1.1. This Directive reissues reference (a).

    1.2. I hereby designate the Secretary of the Army, the Secretary of the Navy, and the
Secretary of the Air Force and, in accordance with the assignment of their duties and
responsibilities, the corresponding Under Secretary and Assistant Secretaries of each Military
Department, to perform, as applicable to their respective Departments, the functions described in
clauses (5), (6), (8), (13), and (14) of section 1 of reference (b) without the approval, ratification,
or other action of the President.


2. EFFECTIVE DATE

This Directive is effective immediately.
