SUBJECT:       Delegation of Authority Pursuant to Executive Order 10621

References: (a) DoD Directive 5100.9, “Delegation of Authority Pursuant to Executive
            Order 10621,” September 22, 1955 (hereby canceled)
            (b) Executive Order 10621, “Delegation of Certain Functions of the President to
                the Secretary of Defense,” July 1, 1955, as amended

1. REISSUANCE AND PURPOSE
   1.1. This Directive reissues Reference (a).

    1.2. Pursuant to Section 1 of Reference (b), I designate the Secretary of the Navy and, in
accordance with the assignment of their respective duties and responsibilities, the Under
Secretary or the Navy and the Assistant Secretaries of the Navy, to perform with respect to the
Department of the Navy the functions of the President described in Sections 1(a), 1(c), 1(g), and
1(h) of Executive Order 10621, without the approval, ratification, or other action of the
President.


2. EFFECTIVE DATE

This Directive is effective immediately.
