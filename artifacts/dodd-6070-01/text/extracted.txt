               SECTION 1: GENERAL ISSUANCE INFORMATION

1.1. APPLICABILITY.

  a. This issuance applies to OSD, the Military Departments, the Defense Agencies, and the
DoD Field Activities.

    b. This issuance also applies to the United States Coast Guard, the Commissioned Corps of
the Public Health Service, and the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration when the
Secretaries of the Departments of Homeland Security, and Health and Human Services enter into
agreements with the Secretary of Defense for the uniformed services under their respective
jurisdictions to participate in the Fund, pursuant to Chapter 56 of Title 10, United States Code.


1.2. POLICY.

Funding for health care programs for Medicare-eligible beneficiaries will be in accordance with
the provisions of Chapter 56 of Title 10, United States Code.


1.3. INFORMATION COLLECTIONS.

The accounting system source documents, output reports, and notifications required for the
operation of the Fund, are exempt from licensing in accordance with Volume 1 of DoD
Manual 8910.01.

                           SECTION 2: RESPONSIBILITIES

2.1. DOD ENTITIES WITH DOD MEDICARE ELIGIBLE RETIREE HEALTH CARE
FUND CONCERNS.

DoD entities with DoD Medicare Eligible Retiree Health Care Fund concerns, as defined in the
Glossary, perform responsibilities related to the Fund as assigned in Chapter 16 of Volume 12 of
DoD 7000.14-R.


2.2. UNDER SECRETARY OF DEFENSE FOR PERSONNEL AND READINESS.

In addition to the responsibilities in Paragraph 2.1., the Under Secretary of Defense for Personnel
and Readiness, issues and revises portions of Chapter 16 of Volume 12 of DoD 7000.14-R
pertaining to the Fund in conjunction with the Under Secretary of Defense (Comptroller)/Chief
Financial Officer, Department of Defense.


2.3. UNDER SECRETARY OF DEFENSE (COMPTROLLER)/CHIEF FINANCIAL
OFFICER OF THE DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE.

In addition to the responsibilities in Paragraph 2.1., the Under Secretary of Defense
(Comptroller)/Chief Financial Officer of the Department of Defense issues and revises portions
of Chapter 16 of Volume 12 of DoD 7000.14-R pertaining to the Fund in conjunction with the
Under Secretary of Defense for Personnel and Readiness.
