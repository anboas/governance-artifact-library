SUBJECT:      Revitalizing Base Closure Communities and Community Assistance - Community
              Redevelopment and Homeless Assistance

References: See Enclosure


1. PURPOSE. This instruction:

    a. Reissues DoD Instruction 4165.68 (Reference (a)) in accordance with the authority in
DoD Directive 5134.01 (Reference (b)) to establish policy and assign responsibilities for
implementing the community-based process that addresses the needs of the homeless at base
closure and realignment sites pursuant to Public Law 103-421 (Reference (c)).

   b. Provides procedures for identifying interest from State and local entities for property
under a public benefit transfer.

   c. Gives guidance to the Military Departments pursuant to the laws and regulations cited in
Reference (c) and Public Laws 101-510 and 100-526 (References (d) and (e)).


2. APPLICABILITY

   a. This instruction applies to:

        (1) The Office of the Secretary of Defense, the Military Departments, the Chairman of
the Joint Chiefs of Staff, the Combatant Commands, the Defense Agencies, and the DoD Field
Activities (referred to in this instruction as “the DoD Components”).

      (2) All installations that are approved for closure or realignment by the President and
Congress pursuant to Reference (d) after October 25, 1994.

   b. The Local Redevelopment Authority is responsible for identifying interest from homeless
providers and State and local entities interested in property under a public benefit transfer.
Requirements of the Local Redevelopment Authority, the general public, and the Department of
                                                              DoDI 4165.68, February 13, 2014


Housing and Urban Development are stated in Part 176 of Title 32, Code of Federal Regulations
(Reference (f)).


3. POLICY. It is DoD policy that communities impacted by base closures and realignments
achieve rapid economic recovery through the reuse of the assets of closing bases in accordance
with this instruction and DoD 4165.66-M (Reference (g)). This will be accomplished by
ensuring that communities and the Military Departments communicate effectively and work
together to accomplish mutual goals of quick property disposal and rapid job generation.


4. RESPONSIBILITIES

   a. Deputy Under Secretary of Defense for Installations and Environment (DUSD(I&E)).
Under the authority, direction, and control of the Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition,
Technology, and Logistics and in coordination with the General Counsel of the Department of
Defense and other officials as appropriate, the DUSD(I&E) develops and distributes guidance to
implement laws, directives, and instructions on the retention or disposal of real and personal
property at closing or realigning bases.

   b. DoD Component Heads. DoD Component heads ensure compliance with this instruction
and guidance issued by the DUSD(I&E) on revitalizing base closure communities.


5. PROCEDURES. Procedures are detailed in Reference (f).


6. RELEASABILITY. Cleared for public release. This instruction is available on the
Directives Division Website at https://www.esd.whs.mil/DD/.


7. SUMMARY OF CHANGE 2. The change to this issuance updates references and removes
expiration language in accordance with current Chief Management Officer of the Department of
Defense direction.
