                            DOD INSTRUCTION 5025.14
    DOD GUIDANCE FOR IMPLEMENTATION OF THE NATIONAL
           VESSEL OF INTEREST (NVOI) LEXICON

Originating Component:       Office of the Under Secretary of Defense for Policy

Effective:                   August 24, 2018

Releasability:               Cleared for public release. Available on the Directives Division Website
                             at http://www.esd.whs.mil/DD/.

Approved by:                 John C. Rood, Under Secretary of Defense for Policy


Purpose: In accordance with the authority in DoD Directive 5111.1, the November 30, 2006, Deputy
Secretary of Defense Memorandum, and DoD Directive 2005.02E, this issuance establishes policy for the
implementation of executive branch guidance for sharing of vessel of interest (VOI) information among
DoD Components in accordance with the National Maritime Domain Awareness Plan for the National
Strategy for Maritime Security (NMDAP).

               SECTION 1: GENERAL ISSUANCE INFORMATION

1.1. APPLICABILITY. This issuance applies to OSD, the Military Departments (including the
Coast Guard at all times, including when it is a Service in the Department of Homeland Security
by agreement with that Department), the Office of the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff and
the Joint Staff, the Combatant Commands, the Office of the Inspector General of the Department
of Defense, the Defense Agencies, the DoD Field Activities, and all other organizational entities
within DoD (referred to collectively in this issuance as the “DoD Components”).


1.2. POLICY. The NVOI Lexicon, as established in Appendix F of the NMDAP, will be
implemented broadly across the Department. This standardized categorization guidance
minimizes confusion, facilitates rapid and shared understanding of potential threats of VOIs, and
maximizes intelligence and information sharing among departments, agencies, and mission
partners.

                           SECTION 2: RESPONSIBILITIES

2.1. UNDER SECRETARY OF DEFENSE FOR POLICY (USD(P)). The USD(P):

   a. In coordination with the Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence (USD(I)), oversees
and provides policy guidance to the DoD Executive Agent (EA) for Maritime Domain
Awareness (MDA).

    b. Oversees compatibility of DoD MDA efforts with national MDA policy and
interoperability, as appropriate, with other MDA efforts by other nations and international
organizations.

   c. In coordination with the DoD EA for MDA, develops, coordinates, and publishes
guidance on the roles, responsibilities, authorities, and relationships for the DoD EA for MDA
and associated MDA stakeholders.


2.2. USD(I). The USD(I), in accordance with DoD Directive 2005.02E, represents the DoD EA
for MDA and the USD(P) to the Director of National Intelligence on DoD-related MDA matters.
USD(I) will provide supplemental maritime intelligence guidance, as necessary, to Defense
Intelligence Components to implement this issuance.


2.3. DOD COMPONENT HEADS. DoD Component heads must revise existing policies, or
develop new policies, as necessary, to adopt and:

   a. Integrate the NVOI Lexicon into the component’s existing information management or
knowledge management plans.

  b. Implement collaborative information and intelligence exchanges with other DoD
Components, federal departments and agencies, and foreign mission partners.


2.4. SECRETARY OF THE NAVY. In addition to the responsibilities in Paragraph 2.3., the
Secretary of the Navy, in his or her capacity as the DoD EA for MDA:

    a. Oversees execution of MDA initiatives within DoD and coordinates on MDA policy with
the USD(P).

   b. Ensures continuous, global, and sustainable support for DoD MDA implementation is
coordinated with the DoD Components.

   c. Develops and distributes goals, objectives, and desired effects for MDA relevant to DoD
missions in coordination with the USD(P) and the USD(I).

                                        GLOSSARY

G.1. ACRONYMS.

 EA                      executive agent

 MDA                     maritime domain awareness

 NMDAP                   National Maritime Domain Awareness Plan for the National Strategy
                         for Maritime Security
 NVOI                    national vessel of interest

 USD(P)                  Under Secretary of Defense for Policy
 USD(I)                  Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence

 VOI                     vessel of interest


G.2. DEFINITIONS. Unless otherwise noted, these terms and their definitions are for the
purpose of this issuance.

EA. Defined in DoD Directive 5101.1.

DoD MDA. Defined in DoD Directive 2005.02E.

MDA. Defined in the NMDAP.

mission partner. Those with whom the DoD cooperates to achieve national goals, such as other
departments and agencies of the U.S. Government; State and local governments; allies, coalition
members, host nations, and other nations; multinational organizations; non-governmental
organizations; and the private sector.

VOI. Vessels that may pose a national security, law enforcement, or regulatory threat to the
United States.
